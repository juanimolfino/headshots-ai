## 1. How Credits Work

The Service runs on credits:

- **Gold credits** are used to train a model.
- **Blue credits** are used to generate and edit images.

Credits are a **digital, consumable product**. Because they are consumed immediately when you use a feature, they are generally **non-refundable** once purchased, except as described below.

## 2. Failed Attempts — Automatic Credit Return

If a training or generation attempt **fails due to an error on our side or our providers' side** and you do not receive your result, the credits used for that specific attempt are **automatically returned to your balance**. You do not need to request this; it happens as part of how the Service handles failures.

If you believe credits were not correctly returned after a failed attempt, contact us at **[SUPPORT EMAIL]** and we will review it.

## 3. Subscriptions

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account.

- Cancellation stops future billing — **you will not be charged for the next period.**
- **We do not refund subscription fees already paid** for the current period.
- You keep access and any remaining **subscription credits until the end of the current paid period**. Subscription credits **do not carry over** to any later period and are forfeited when the period ends.
- In short: the credits included in the month you already paid for are yours to use **during that month**; they are not refunded and do not roll over.

## 4. Credit Packs

One-time credit packs are **non-refundable** once purchased, because credits are a consumable digital product. Unused pack credits do not expire and remain available until used or until you delete your account.

## 5. Exceptions and Legal Rights

Nothing in this policy limits any **non-waivable statutory rights** you may have as a consumer in your country of residence. If mandatory local law grants you a refund right that this policy does not cover, that law prevails.

We may, at our discretion, grant goodwill refunds in exceptional cases; doing so does not create an obligation to do so again.

For any refund or billing question, contact **[SUPPORT EMAIL]**. Please include your account email and details of the transaction or failed attempt.
